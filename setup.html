Oscilloscope Online

Oscilloscope Online is a web serial plotter, an alternative to Arduino IDE's serial plotter. Feature-rich, clean UI, better charts and tables. It is suitable for data visualization in research work, sensor analysis and plain hobbyist-robotics. It's not limited to Arduino! It can work with any serial device including ESP32, STM and Raspbberry Pi!
